---
title: "Steamtools Settings Screen: Enable Experimental Features"
description: "Access the Settings screen to toggle experimental Mods and Plugins features. Settings are persisted automatically across sessions using eframe storage."
---

The Settings screen is the control panel for optional and experimental Steamtools features. Currently it exposes two checkboxes that show or hide the **Mods** and **Plugins** toolbar buttons. Your choices are saved automatically and restored the next time you launch the application.

## Open Settings

Click the **⚙** button in the top-right corner of the main toolbar. Steamtools switches from the Main Menu view to the Settings screen.

## Available options

| Setting | Default | Effect |
|---------|---------|--------|
| **Mods feature (Experimental)** | Off | Shows the **🔁 Mods** button in the toolbar |
| **Plugins feature (Experimental)** | Off | Shows the **🔌 Plugins** button in the toolbar |

Check a box to enable the corresponding feature. The toolbar updates immediately — you do not need to restart the application.

<Note>
Both features are marked **Experimental**. They may behave unexpectedly or change in future releases.
</Note>

## Return to the Main Menu

Click **↩ Back** at the bottom of the Settings screen to return to the Main Menu.

## Persistence

Settings are saved via eframe's built-in storage mechanism (serialized as JSON). They persist across application restarts. If you update to a new version of Steamtools and the version changes, stored data is cleared and you will need to re-configure your settings.
